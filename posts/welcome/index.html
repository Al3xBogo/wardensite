Welcome to the WardenSMP website!

2023-06-14 :: _A13X

WardenSMP is a Minecraft Java survival server with plenty to do! We are starting our 4th season on June 16th, and plan to stick with the vanilla survival game!
The website has been made pink to match with the new Cherry Grove biome in the 1.20 Trails & Tales Update!

Please note that this is a closed sever, so only people that know the owner or have been invited by a member are permitted to join.
To join, please contact _A13x. If you are a member or have been invited, please join our Google Chat groups to receive announcements and to communicate. Ask _A13X to invite you.
Contact _A13X either directly or using alexbogo2007@protonmail.com.

Also check out our rules and guideliness!
